Valentines Season 1 Episode 1


(Somewhere in LA, at evening, Ryan's House)

Ciana: Mom, Dad it's time for holiday.

Whitby: (Ciana's Mom). Yes that's true, should we travel to New York City.

Ciana: (Excited). Mom how did you know? I was about to say that.

Whitby: Ciana, that's what you always say.

Ryan: (Ciana's Dad). I think that will be lovely, lets get ready tomorrow.

Ciana: (Hugs him). Thanks Dad (Goes to her room). I am so so happy, by tomorrow I will start packing. I am so so happy this needs to be written in my diary.
